## What is JustDare?

JustDare is a decentralized Web3 platform where Creators set challenges, Participants prove their courage, and Viewers decide who wins the bounty — all powered by smart contracts on the Polygon blockchain. Participants compete for POL bounties, viewers pay to watch and vote on performances via peer-to-peer media streaming, and top performers earn on-chain NFT trophies. No middlemen, no centralized servers storing your data — just code enforcing fair play.

---

## Features

- **Create Dares:** Set a title, description, bounty in POL, viewer price, and end date — the smart contract holds funds in decentralized escrow.
- **Participate:** Join public dares as a participant, record your entry, and stream it peer-to-peer to verified viewers via WebRTC.
- **Watch & Vote:** Pay the viewer fee to watch participant performances, then cast your vote to decide who wins the bounty.
- **NFT Trophies:** Earn soulbound NFT trophies (Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum) minted on-chain for your dare achievements.
- **Peer-to-Peer Streaming:** All media is encrypted locally, streamed directly between devices via WebRTC — no cloud storage, no CDN.
- **Secure & Private:** Two-password system (Local Account + Wallet), biometric authentication, zero personal data collection. Keys never leave your device.
- **Multi-Wallet Support:** Create or import multiple wallets, switch accounts, and manage funds — all offline-first.
